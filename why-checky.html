    • Why Use Checky?

    Checksums are more important than you think. Every file has a unique checksum. When you download or share a new file, it is likely that the file may get tampered with or damaged. Comparing checksums will ensure that file in your system is the original one and not damaged. However, checkums are long to read and compare. Their case doesn't matter. That is exactlt why we created Checky. It is a tool which compares your inputs of the checksums(lower or upper case doesn't matter), compares them to check if they are the same and tells you the result. This tool is going to save you a lot of time and energy!

    • Its free- Best things in life are free. So is Checky! We charge you nothing for downloading and using Checky.
    • Its lightweight-Checky won't take more than a few MBs on your computer. It definitely wouldn't be a memory hog.
    • Its open source- The source code of Checky is open source on Github and free for anyone to inspect. You can even use it to create your own software and even contribute to it! Find it here.
    • Freedom- Everything is cleared for personal and commercial use, even the code. We don't take away your freedom to use, install and distribute it.
    • It's just the best- It just it is. No explanation needed!
