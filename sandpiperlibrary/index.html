Manage your personal collection of books easily

The installer works on Windows only


Sandpiper Library allows you to add, remove and manage lending of books in your personal library. You can search through books and authors as well and add members to whom books can be issued. It also keeps track of books that have been lent. Using the app is simple and intuitive.

  • add and remove books and genres.
  • edit book information
  • add and removed members
  • edit member information
  • issue books
  • keep track of books that have been lent
  • search through books
  • import and export data

To use Sandpiper Library, simply open up Sandpiper Library by selecting the app from the Start Menu or searching it on your system. Sandpiper Library's interface will guide you on.

Use the menu on the top to perform various functions. To edit or delete members, genres and books, go to 'Members', 'Genres' and 'Library' pages respectively using the top menu.

Sandpiper Library is a tool that can be used to manage a personal library. You can add, remove and edit information for books and members(or your friends and family!). Genres can be added and deleted. You can keep track of books issued/lent by you. You can even export and import data in case you switch devices/ reinstall the app. This free and open source software distributed under a permissive license may be used by smaaller libraries as well.
