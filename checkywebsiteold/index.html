    What is Checky?

    Checksums to files are what fingerprints are to humans and tongueprints to dogs! They carry the file's integrity. Often ignored, they are important to keep your system and your files secure. Checksums are often long to read and even harder to compare. With Checky you can easily comapare long checksums and check the integrity of your downloaded and transferred files.





    How does it work?

    The programme converts your input to lowercase and compare the long checksums. It will output whether the file is damaged or not. Simple as that. Moreover it is open source and free. It is extremely lightweight and won't hog your memory. Neither it will invade your privacy!





    Checky
    Checky is a free and open source tool that can make your life easier. It compares checksums and tells you the integrity status of your file. Comparing two checksums is the hardest thing to do. That is why we have created this tool for you. Don't bash your head. Just input the checksums and let Checky work its magic! Download now!
